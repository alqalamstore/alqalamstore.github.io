Our Vision

Hello, I'm Suhail Ahmad Tantry, Founder of Al Qalam – The Write Point.

Our vision is simple yet meaningful: we believe that every child deserves the opportunity to learn, regardless of their financial background. No student should be deprived of education because of the cost of books or stationery.

That is why we are committed to offering quality stationery and educational essentials at fair prices, with special discounts for students whenever possible. We strive to support every family and make learning more affordable for everyone.

At Al Qalam – The Write Point, we are not just serving customers—we are investing in education, building trust, and contributing to a brighter future for our community.

— Suhail Ahmad Tantry
